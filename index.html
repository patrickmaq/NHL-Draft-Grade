Context factors

League difficulty
SHL, Liiga, and NCAA skaters score higher on raw attribute grades. MHL and NTDP prospects carry more development uncertainty.
Team Status
Rebuilding vs. contending
Rebuilding teams get +2 on high-need picks — ceiling matters more. Contending teams are graded on floor and readiness.
Overall draft grade is the simple average of all pick scores. The final score is capped at 100 and floored at 0 — value and fit adjustments can push either direction.
